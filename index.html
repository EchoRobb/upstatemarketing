About Us

Welcome to Upstate Marketing, a dynamic marketing agency based in the charming county town of Trowbridge, Wiltshire, United Kingdom. Since our inception on February 28th, 2024, we have proudly helped over 50 businesses achieve remarkable success. Our mission is simple: to prepare businesses for success and guide like-minded entrepreneurs to reach their full potential.

At Upstate Marketing, our dedicated team of 15 professionals combines expertise and creativity to help businesses worldwide achieve their marketing goals. We craft tailored strategies that highlight your brand's unique story, ensuring it stands out in the market. Our services include innovative digital marketing, from SEO and PPC to social media management and content marketing, leveraging the latest tools to drive traffic, engagement, and conversions. Our team excels in developing visually stunning brand identities, including logo design, website development, and graphic design. Additionally, we use data to inform our marketing strategies, ensuring impactful and measurable results. Partner with Upstate Marketing to transform your business into a leading brand. Contact us today to start your journey to success.
